Cao Jewelry is created by Ariadna Mavila in Toronto, Canada. Inspired by archaeology, childhood mischief, and heritage-infused storytelling, each piece reflects the ethos of an artistic practice rooted in visual arts.

Hand-carved, cast, and finished in Toronto using recycled silver, Cao combines bold, sculptural designs with ethical, circular production methods.

With a background in fine arts and a passion for craftsmanship, every Cao piece is designed to last a lifetime, prioritizing longevity over fast jewelry trends. Produced in small batches, embracing sustainable materials and meaningful design.
